Meet Zeger

Hey, I’m Zeger

Welcome to my digital corner! I’m currently studying Graduaat Programmeren at Arteveldehogeschool. I’m passionate about how code, creativity, and motion come together to build experiences that are both intuitive and visually engaging. Code’s chaotic, but the pixels are thriving.

From designing smooth web animations to structuring clear and maintainable code, I enjoy bringing ideas to life, one line at a time.

My Developer Side

As a student and aspiring web developer, I’m expanding my skill set with front-end experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, using GSAP for animations and exploring Rive, Spline, TypeScript, and Angular. I’m currently learning React and Vite to strengthen my workflow.

On the back end, I have experience with Craft CMS, Twig, Node.js, and Express, and I’m currently deepening my knowledge of PHP with Laravel, Filament, and SQL to build full-stack applications.

Outside the Code

I’m into sports, adventure, and spontaneous road trips, but I also love slow, cozy evenings at home. I’m a bit of a homebody at heart, and I love sharing quiet moments and simple joys with my girlfriend. Whether it's cooking, watching a series, or just talking, that time means a lot to me.
